@ How much is the rent per semester?

- We only provide 6 bed housings, and it costs $1100 for Spring, Fall, and Winter semesters, and $500 for summer.

@ What kinds of amenities do they provide?

- We offer a modern living spaces, a gym, on-site washers and dryers, study spaces, two refridgerators in each apartment, etc. For more information, please see “Amenities” section.

@ How does an apartment look like?

- Each apartment comes with two refridgerators, on-site washer and dryer, two bathrooms, etc. (For more information, please see “Floor Plans” section.)

@ How long does it take to get to the campus?

- It takes about 5 minutes to the stadium, and 8 minutes to the I-Center.

@ How many people do I have to live with?

- We only offer shared room apartments. You will have two options: 6 people housing, and 8 people housing.

@ @ How do I pay my rent?

- You can choose to pay either with cash or online (e-check or credit card)

@ Do you have a discount for multi-semester contacts?

- Yes. You will have $50 off in each semester for signing two or more contracts in one application.

@ Do I need to pay an application fee every semester?

- No, you don't. You only pay the application fee for the first semester you sign.

@ Can I bring my pet?

- Our apartments are not allowed to have pets.

@ What are the office hours?

- The office hours are: Mon - Fri: 9am-3pm Sat: 10am - 1pm Sun: Closed
