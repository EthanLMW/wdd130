Content

Home page

Purpose - Students are always looking for a place that is comfortable, convenient, and affordable. They will be searching on the internet to find one that meets their needs the most. From our experiences, when we go on the websites of other apartments, it is very difficult to get all the information I want quickly. We often had to look through every small thing to find them, and it took too much time to figure out what is where. Your search to find the apartment you want brought you to the perfect place. You will find it intriguing as you explore our site because it is designed for students to provide information which most students are looking for. In addition, you will be able to search and know about our apartments easily and quickly. Our website will be simple enough for students to have an easy access to all that we have to offer. Floorplan - One of the most important part of a housing is about how comfortable and convenient the students feel in the apartment. At RI Aparments, students will be able to feel like being at home. Our apartments are designed just to provide the best environment for you. With all that we have in apartments, you will be able to focus on academics. As a BYU-Idaho approved housing and in association with BYU-Idaho, we promote the Student Living Standards and encourage students to live by them. Amenities - We offer a variety of amenities for you to have a more enjoyable experience at our apartment. You can mingle with your friends at our clubhouse, exercise in the fitness center, or study at the study room when you want a quiet environment to study.

FAQ

Our website can offer answers to the following questions: @ How much is the rent per semester? - We only provide 6 bed housings, and it costs $1100 for Spring, Fall, and Winter semesters, and $500 for summer. @ What kinds of amenities do they provide? - We offer a modern living spaces, a gym, on-site washers and dryers, study spaces, two refridgerators in each apartment, etc. For more information, please see “Amenities” section. @ How long does it take to get to the campus? - It takes about 5 minutes to the stadium, and 8 minutes to the I-Center. @ How does an apartment look like? - Each apartment comes with two refridgerators, on-site washer and dryer, two bathrooms, etc. (For more information, please see “Floor Plans” section.) @ How many people do I have to live with? - We only offer shared room apartments. You will have two options: 6 people housing, and 8 people housing. @ How do I pay my rent? - You can choose to pay either with cash or online (e-check or credit card) @ Do you have a discount for multi-semester contacts? - Yes. You will have $50 off in each semester for signing two or more contracts in one application. @ Do I need to pay an application fee every semester? - No, you don't. You only pay the application fee for the first semester you sign. @ Can I bring my pet? - Our apartments are not allowed to have pets. @ What are the office hours? - The office hours are: Mon - Fri: 9am-3pm Sat: 10am - 1pm Sun: Closed
